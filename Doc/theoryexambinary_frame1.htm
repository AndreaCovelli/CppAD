A Binary Representation of the Procedure
We define the variable  s_0 (x) = a_n for all  x \in \R and for  i = 1, \ldots , n , we define  s_i : \R \rightarrow \R and  t_i : \R \rightarrow \R by:  \[
\begin{array}{rcl}
     t_i (x) & = & s_{i-1} (x) \; x             \\
     s_i (x) & = & t_i (x) + a_{n - i}
\end{array}
\]  It follows that  s_n(x) and  t_n(x) are equal to the values of the variables s and t on the n-th iteration of the loop in the example procedure .
